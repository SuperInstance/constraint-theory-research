### Reviewer Anticipation

#### Expected Strengths

**Theoretical Contributions:**
- Rigorous mathematical framework
- Novel geometric approach
- Complete complexity analysis
- Theorem proofs

**Experimental Validation:**
- Comprehensive simulation results
- Multiple hardware platforms
- Statistical significance
- Reproducible methodology

**Practical Impact:**
- Clear performance improvements
- Real-world applications
- Open-source implementation
- Deployment roadmap

#### Potential Weaknesses (and Rebuttals)

**Concern: Theoretical Complexity**
- **Rebuttal:** Mathematical rigor is necessary for deterministic guarantees
- Provide intuitive explanations alongside formal proofs
- Include visualizations and examples

**Concern: Hardware Dependencies**
- **Rebuttal:** Adaptive approach works across platforms
- Provide CPU-only fallback
- Show results on multiple hardware configurations

**Concern: Scalability Limits**
- **Rebuttal:** O(log n) complexity ensures scalability
- Provide large-scale experimental validation
- Theoretical bounds on performance

**Concern: Practical Deployment**
- **Rebuttal:** Paper 3 addresses deployment in detail
- Provide case studies from production systems
- Discuss engineering challenges and solutions
