## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks

1. **Scope Creep**
   - **Mitigation:** Strict page limits, clear definitions
   - **Trigger:** Page count exceeds 150

2. **Quality Issues**
   - **Mitigation:** Peer review, testing requirements
   - **Trigger:** Inconsistent style, broken examples

3. **Timeline Slippage**
   - **Mitigation:** Buffer time, priority ordering
   - **Trigger:** Missed weekly targets

4. **Resource Constraints**
   - **Mitigation:** Community contributions, phased approach
   - **Trigger:** Team unavailable

### Contingency Plans

- **If behind schedule:** Focus on high-priority pages first
- **If quality issues:** Implement stricter review process
- **If resource shortage:** Recruit community contributors
